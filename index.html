About Me

Who is Emily? I am a web developer and programmer from the Lake Cumberland area of Kentucky. I have a beautiful family and have been blessed to spend the majority of the last few years at home with my children. Fast forward to 2020, I enrolled in college and landed in web development.

At my core I am a people person with a hunger for growth; both personal and professional. I am excited to be a part of a field that is growing exponentially and setting the standard for the digital age we live in.

In web development, I learn quickly and the creative in me loves seeing my work come to life. My goal is to spend every day of my life continuing to learn and grow and never have to look back and wish that I had.
